Sixth month

02 Sep 2017

This week the six month of working with the Prototype Fund finally ended.

On Thursday every project could present their work of the last months during the closing event. It wasn’t that easy to pack everything that happend in the last 6 month into a 5 minute presentation, but I think it worked.

@bitboxer is presenting his awesome tool @signdict A sign language wiki 0x1f44c#prototypedemos https://t.co/nFmz25ftWE pic.twitter.com/DsLwn8hZze

— Julia Kloiber (@j_kloiber) August 31, 2017

This was also a great opportunity to meet everyone from the first and second round of the Prototype Fund. Currently the registration for the third round is open. If you have an Idea for an OpenSource project and want to earn 47.500€ for 6 month of work, apply now!

As already mentioned in the last post this month was also the first month of my new full time job. Sadly I didn’t manage to add new features to SignDict because of that. Instead I talked with a lot of people about potential improvements to SignDict.

My pr work also continues. Last month I had a nice interview in German with detektor.fm.

In the next half year I want to concentrate to make SignDict better for people who want to use it as a teaching tool. If you are a teacher for Sign Language, please contact me. I would love to talk to you about your needs.
